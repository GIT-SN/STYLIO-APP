# Stylio - Your Ultimate Fashion Destination

Stylio is an innovative clothing and fashion website that offers a curated collection of stylish, high-quality apparel and accessories for men, women, and kids. From casual outfits to formal wear, Stylio caters to a variety of tastes and preferences, ensuring there's something for everyone.

## Features:
- **Wide Variety**: A collection of clothing, shoes, accessories, and more for different occasions.
- **Trendy & Timeless Styles**: Stay up-to-date with the latest fashion trends, while also offering timeless pieces for every wardrobe.
- **User-Friendly Shopping Experience**: Designed with a focus on a seamless, responsive, and easy-to-navigate interface.
- **Quality & Comfort**: Our products are crafted with attention to detail and high standards of quality.

## Tech Stack:
- **Frontend**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **Responsive Design**: Ensures the website is optimized for all devices, from desktops to mobiles.
